Preparation

  1. Start by preparing the sugar syrup; combine the caster sugar and water in a small saucepan. Bring to a simmer over a medium heat, stirring as you do to dissolve the sugar. Once dissolved, continue to simmer for 2-3 minutes to thicken the syrup a little. Remove from the heat and allow to cool to one side.
  2. Meanwhile, purée the raspberries by mashing them in a bowl using a potato masher; alternatively, blend in a food processor until smooth. Press the purée through a fine sieve sat on top of a bowl to collect the purée
